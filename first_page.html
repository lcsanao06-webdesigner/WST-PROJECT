What is YSLEP?

Youth Servant Leadership and Education Program (YSLEP) is a comprehensive and integrated development program designed to strengthen the youth’s knowledge, skills, and character. It aims to cultivate qualities such as self-discipline, moral integrity, leadership, and social responsibility, ensuring that young individuals grow not only in academic excellence but also in values-driven service.

❝Forming Servant Leaders, not just providing scholarships.❞

Caritas Manila recognizes YSLEP as one of the most impactful developmental programs for poverty education. The program is structured into two main phases: the Pre-phase, which involves the selection and identification of potential scholars through the CMIP process, and the Proper Phase, which encompasses capacity building, financial assistance, continuous monitoring and evaluation, formation activities, parish involvement, and regular monthly meetings.

Scholars successfully complete their college, technical, or vocational courses; secure employment or start their own entrepreneurial ventures within six months after graduation; and actively share their 3T’s—Time, Talent, and Treasure—in the Church and in their communities as committed servant leaders.

Program Components


Pre-phase – Caritas Manila Implementing Partner (CMIP) selection and identification.
Proper Phase – Capacity building, financial assistance, monitoring, and evaluation, formation, social apostolate, parish involvement, monthly meeting.


Sustainable Development Goals:

  • No Poverty: YSLEP helps break the cycle of poverty by supporting disadvantaged youth through scholarships, leadership formation, and personal development, empowering them to secure better futures for themselves and their families.
  • Life on Land: YSLEP promotes care for creation through formation activities that encourage environmental responsibility, community clean-ups, and sustainable practices rooted in stewardship and Catholic social teachings.
  • Decent Work and Economic Growth: Through education support, mentoring, and values formation, YSLEP equips scholars with the skills and discipline needed to attain stable employment, contribute to the economy, and uplift their communities.
  • Quality Education: YSLEP provides holistic and values-driven education assistance, ensuring scholars not only finish their studies but also grow as responsible, service-oriented leaders aligned with Caritas Philippines' mission.

Academic Performance:

  • 1 subject deficiency – Probation (P)
  • 2 subjects deficiency – On Hold (H)
  • 3 or more subject deficiencies – Termination (W)

Renewal Policy and Procedure:

  • Individual Evaluation Sheet
  • Copy of Grades with Certified True Copy
  • Index Cards: Parish Involvement, Social Apostolate, Monthly Formation
  • Latest Certificate of Registration/Certificate of Enrollment with Certified True Copy

  • 2 Evaluations (1st and 2nd Semester)
